# Ganitra Product Specification

Ganitra is a mathematics understanding platform whose first product is a textbook series.

Core belief:
Mathematics becomes easy when you can see it.

Target audiences:
1. Parents
2. Students
3. Teachers

Pages:
- Home
- Books
- Sample Chapters
- About
- Contact

Future:
- AI Tutor
- Interactive Practice
- Teacher Resources
- School Programs

Design:
- Modern educational technology company
- Professional but colorful
- Math-inspired visuals
- Not childish
- Not corporate consulting

Tech:
- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind
- Vercel
- Mobile-first
- SEO optimized
